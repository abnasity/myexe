Suicidal thoughts

In rare cases,some people experience suicidal thoughts and a desire to self-harm when they first take anitidepressants.Young people under 25 seem particularly at risk. It may be useful to tell a relative or close friend if youve started antidepressants and ask them to read the leaflet that comes with your medicines. You should then ask them to tell you if they think your symptoms are getting worse, or if theyre worried about changes in your behaviour.
